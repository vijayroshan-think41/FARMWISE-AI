1. Season Overview
Rabi 2026-27 begins on the back of a near-normal Kharif season. Soil moisture levels are adequate across most wheat-growing
states — Punjab, Haryana, Uttar Pradesh, and Madhya Pradesh. Reservoir storage is above average, supporting assured irrigation
for wheat and mustard. Cold wave risk in December–January must be monitored for mustard and cumin.
Key message: Wheat MSP has been increased to ■2,275/qtl — excellent returns expected. Mustard prices remain strong due to
edible oil demand. Onion Rabi planting window is October–November for February–March harvest.
